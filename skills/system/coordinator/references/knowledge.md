# Coordinator Skill — Cross-Device Orchestration Knowledge

## Device Interactions

- AC plus humidifier: AC often dries the air, so humidifier targets may need to rise
- AC plus open windows: cooling or heating becomes wasteful
- Heating plus humidifier: heating dries the air in the same way AC often does
- Lights plus time of day: lighting should stay aligned with circadian expectations
- Purifier plus open windows: purification is less effective

## Orchestration Principles

- Avoid conflicting actions such as simultaneous heating and cooling
- Prefer passive or cheaper adjustments before expensive ones
- Coordinate changes gradually instead of firing many commands at once
- Prioritize safety over comfort and comfort over energy savings
