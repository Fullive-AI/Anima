# Air Conditioner Skill — Domain Knowledge

## Comfortable Temperature Ranges

- General comfort: 22C-26C
- Sleep: 23C-25C because slightly cool air supports sleep
- Summer cooling: 24C-26C
- Winter heating: 20C-23C
- Above 28C: discomfort and productivity drop
- Below 18C: too cold for most users

## Energy Optimization

- Lowering the summer setpoint by 1C can cost roughly 6%-8% more energy
- Keeping indoor temperature within about 8C of outdoor temperature is usually more efficient
- If temperature is only slightly above target, fan or auto mode may be enough
- Avoid frequent on/off cycling near the target range

## Interaction With Other Devices

- AC use often lowers humidity, so humidifier coordination matters
- Open windows make active cooling or heating wasteful
- Night mode should usually reduce fan speed and soften the target
