# Humidifier Skill — Domain Knowledge

## Comfortable Humidity Ranges

- General comfort: 40%-60% relative humidity
- Sleep: 45%-55% because slightly higher humidity can help breathing
- Summer: 40%-50% to avoid sticky air
- Winter: 45%-60% to offset dry heating
- Below 30%: skin dryness, static, respiratory irritation
- Above 70%: mold risk, dust mites, discomfort

## Interaction With Other Factors

- Air conditioning reduces humidity, so humidifier demand may rise while AC is running
- Winter heating dries air quickly, so targets can be slightly higher
- Open windows in already humid weather make humidification wasteful
- Air purifiers can reduce humidity slightly but should not dominate the decision

## Control Strategy

- Adjust targets in 5%-10% steps and allow time to stabilize
- Prefer quiet or silent modes at night
- If water level is below 20%, conserve water and avoid aggressive targets
- Turn off when humidity is already within about 3% of target
